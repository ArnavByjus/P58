Laying down on the warm grass

When the sun is up, and I have some time, I prefer to lay down on the warm grass, but why? Well, it's warm, and we all like warmth, for example: taking a shower, we don't want a cold shower, we want a warm one. So to conclude, I prefer to be in the warm sun on the warm grass.
